About Your Dominant Dosha

{% if dominant_dosha == "Vata" %}

🌬️ Vata — The Energy of Movement

Vata dosha governs movement, creativity, and flexibility. Made of Air and Space, Vata types are naturally quick-thinking, enthusiastic, and adaptable. When balanced, Vata brings vitality and joy. When imbalanced, it can cause anxiety, dryness, irregular digestion, and sleep disturbances. Grounding, warmth, and routine are your best friends.

{% elif dominant_dosha == "Pitta" %}

🔥 Pitta — The Energy of Transformation

Pitta dosha governs metabolism, transformation, and intelligence. Made of Fire and Water, Pitta types are natural leaders — driven, focused, and ambitious. When balanced, Pitta brings sharp intellect and strong digestion. When imbalanced, it can manifest as inflammation, irritability, and burnout. Cooling, moderation, and surrender are your medicine.

{% else %}

🌊 Kapha — The Energy of Stability

Kapha dosha governs structure, stability, and lubrication. Made of Earth and Water, Kapha types are naturally calm, compassionate, and grounded. When balanced, Kapha brings strength, endurance, and unwavering loyalty. When imbalanced, it can cause lethargy, weight gain, and attachment. Movement, stimulation, and lightness are your allies.

{% endif %}
